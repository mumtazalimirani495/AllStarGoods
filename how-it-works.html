AllStarGoods - How It Works

Crypto Payments Made Easy

At AllStarGoods, we proudly accept payments in over 200+ cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, and many more. Whether you're using a popular coin or a niche token, we've got you covered.

You can pay using a secure QR code generated at checkout, or manually by copying the wallet address provided. Both options are fast, reliable, and verified by trusted crypto payment providers.

Why Crypto Only?

We believe in the future of decentralized finance. By accepting crypto only, AllStarGoods ensures faster transactions, lower fees, and global accessibility. No banks, no borders—just seamless shopping powered by blockchain.

Returns & Refunds

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within the first 7 days of delivery. We want you to love what you buy, and we stand by our products.

Refunds are processed within the same 7-day window as per our policy. Once your return is approved, your crypto refund will be issued promptly to the wallet address you provide.

Need Help?

If you have questions about payments, returns, or anything else, our support team is here for you. Just head over to our Contact Us page and we’ll assist you as quickly as possible.

All payments you make are safe & secure by verified crypto providers.
